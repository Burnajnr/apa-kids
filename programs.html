Curriculum Overview

Planned Academic Programs

6 Weeks – 14 Months Infant Development

Infant Development Program

Our infant program will provide a gentle, responsive environment that mirrors the warmth of home. We focus on sensory exploration, building emotional security, physical motor skills, and primary language sounds.

Clean, sanitized sensory areas Multi-sensory developmental toys Attentive and responsive care Daily development logging
15 Months – 2 Years Toddler Foundations

Toddler Foundations Program

As children start moving and exploring, our toddler program channels their natural energy into foundational concepts. We introduce basic vocabulary, color recognition, social play, sharing, and fine motor coordinate exercises.

Vocabulary & speech exercises Socialization & play activities Color, shape, and block building Coordinated motor development
3 Years – 5 Years Preschool Readiness

Preschool Readiness Program

Our preschool curriculum is engineered to prepare children for kindergarten and elementary structures. We build early math logical reasoning, problem-solving skills, vocabulary, phonics, and independence.

Basic math & number concepts Writing preparation & pencil grip Phonics & reading comprehension Independent problem-solving
All Age Levels Core Literacy

Early Literacy Development

We prioritize language immersion across all programs. By integrating daily reading sessions, storytelling circles, word walls, and letter games, we foster an authentic, organic interest in books and writing early.

Daily circle storytelling Letter sounds & vocabulary walls Interactive nursery rhymes Rich visual classroom library
All Age Levels Acdemic Standard

Structured Early Learning Curriculum

Instead of unstructured babysitting, our academy delivers clear, daily lesson structures mapped to childhood cognitive standards. Each day features balanced segments of logical learning, physical play, rest, and artistic creation.

Mapped developmental lessons Balanced structured / free play Art, music & creative circles Multi-sensory learning stations
All Age Levels Social Growth

Social & Emotional Development

We focus intensely on emotional intelligence. Children learn to identify and speak about their feelings, resolve classroom conflicts with peer empathy, share resources, and practice self-regulation techniques.

Conflict resolution methods Cooperative circle games Emotional regulation checks Confidence building exercises
Proposed Feature Family Convenience

Weekend & Extended Hour Care (Planned)

Understanding the complexities of modern job schedules, we plan to provide extended morning, evening, and weekend slots. This option will be available to help parents maintain work-life balance without sacrificing premium care quality.

Planned weekend care availability Late pick-up and early drop-off Flexible program scheduling Healthy planned evening snack plans
Diverse Abilities Inclusion Focus

Inclusive Learning Focus

We are dedicated to building a welcoming early learning experience that welcomes children of all cognitive, behavioral, physical, and developmental abilities.

Adaptive learning materials Multi-sensory classroom designs Professional teacher training Individualized growth tracking
